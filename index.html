My Blog

Taipei City Experience

During my time in Taipei, I delved into the city's rich tapestry of religions and temples. I embarked on three enlightening field trips to Taiwan's iconic temples, immersing myself in diverse religious practices, from polytheism to pantheism. I was captivated by how locals invoked gods for various needs, and I deepened my understanding of Buddhism, Taoism, and Confucianism. At the Beiguan Ensemble temple, I experienced the resonating drum rituals. The Guandu Festival was a highlight, contrasting vividly with my own Islamic beliefs. My final exploration was a self-led visit to the Temple near NTU, where I observed local religious practices firsthand. This Taipei journey expanded my horizons, fostering a profound respect for diverse beliefs.

Learning Korean in S(e)oul

One of my cherished hobbies is diving into the intricacies of different languages. In Seoul, this passion took a vibrant turn as I embarked on a journey to learn Korean, collaborating with friends at a dedicated space. Within a span of three months, I mastered conversational Korean and became proficient in reading the Hangeul alphabet. This linguistic endeavor was beautifully intertwined with my love for Korean K-pop culture. The pinnacle of this experience was attending a concert of my favorite group, Stray Kids. The electrifying atmosphere and the connection with the music made it an unforgettable highlight of my time in Korea.
